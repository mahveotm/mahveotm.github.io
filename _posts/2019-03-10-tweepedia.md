Can we make a change?

Couple of times on Twitter, we stumble upon a new word, a new name or something/someone we just haven't heard (about) before.
As a routine, what we do is exit twitter, check the word on Google, and then probably read up more on Wikipedia.


What if there was a bot that responds to mentions and give you snippets of the subject you intend to serch for from Wikipedia.
It would also provide a link should you desire to read further without you leaving Twitter!

![Dang!](/img/screenshot3_1552252418057.png)

I call it Tweepedia (culled from twitter and Wikipedia) and work in an essentially simple fashion. It reads up mentions and check the content with the REST API Wikipedia provides. It then returns a snippet of the atricle (in 240 words considering twitter character limit) and the continue reading link.
