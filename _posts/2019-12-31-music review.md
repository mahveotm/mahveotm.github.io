I'm also a very huge music fan, and if the genre makes sense, be sure that I'm down for it.

Now here's my Apple music vs Spotify post for a regular guy, using an android phone in the city of Lagos, Nigeria.


**[Beat One](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beats_1)** : You can say *[Mahd oh!](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=Mad%20Oh)* That's my favorite radio station (apologies to Beat FM 99.9, I won some movie tickets with those guys and they're my second best). From [Julie Adenuga](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julie_Adenuga) (I really love the way she pronounces the name), to Zane Lowe, to Ibro and the top music countdown, it's really an amazing station.  
Apple says it's the most listened to radio station in the world and that's undisputable! I've also heard some amazing artise/band/ that I've grown to really live(Highwaywoman, WSTRN, Santi). This is really the beauty of Apple Music. Unfortunately, Spotify does not have the equivalent of live radio, so just live with it man, just do.

**Speed**: I'm kinda a tech enthusiast so speed in terms of seconds means a lot to me, I'm also very particular if a website is fast or slow, which is something that has spilled over to my music. Apple Music is very slow on andoid. I won 't be definite on that one since I use an Andriod device, but compared to Spotify, you might need some seconds before your music starts playing. on Spotify, your music starts playing almost immediately without any buffering. [I heard it's some romance between Python, Java and C++](https://labs.spotify.com/2013/03/20/how-we-use-python-at-spotify/) that makes Spotify so fast, but again i have not been able to confirm this!


**Recommended listens**: Spotify does a great job with this one. I can -and have listened to music non-stop (for over 12 hours -don't ask me what I ws doing) with amazing recommended music that I actually loved listening to. Apple music recommended listen is trash, let's leave it like that.

**Cost**: That's the most important one right. In Nigeria for me as a student, Apple music is far cheaper and a no brainer , compared to Spotify. I get charged ₦450.00 per month with my first 3 months absolutely free. On Spotify, I get to pay $9 per month to be able to enjoy downloads and other benefits of Pro. Spotify allows you to listen to music for free interrupted with ads and multiple functions like replay, downloads or skip removed or limited.
My first 3 or 4 months on Spotify, I actually paid the $9, till I spoke with a friend from Belgium whom I met on twitter that allowed me to use her ID while she uses my Netflix passcode. That way, I won't have to use an escrow and also save cost, needing to subscribe to only Netflix. 
That went on for some month before I switched back to my account and continued with the free tier - till now.

So it's a closely fought out war, but if you're in Nigeria, and can afford to cough out ₦450 monthly, Apple music is a no brainer, otherwise --- notjustok.com
