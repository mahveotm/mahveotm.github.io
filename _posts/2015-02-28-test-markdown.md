Let's start by why I'm (un)qualified to do this review. 
I have a total listening hours of over 900hours listening to music on Apple music and over 600 hours listen hours on Spotify.

![Dang!](/img/Apple_music.png)
i'm a huge misic fan, and if the genre make sense, I'm down for it.

Now here's my Apple music vs Spotify post for an regular guy, using an andriod phone in the city of Lagos, Nigeria.


Beat One : You can say Mahd oh! That's my fav radio station every(apologies to Beat FM 99.9 I won some movie tickets with those guys and theyre my second best). From Julie Adenuga(I really love the way she pronounces the name) -btw, did you know she's Skepta's elder sister?, to Zayne Lowe and the top music countdown, it's really an amazing stations. Stats by Apple says it's the most listened to radio station in the world and rightly so. I've also heard some amazing songs that I've grown to really live(Highwaywoman, WSTRN, xyz). That's really the beauty of apple music. Unfortunately Spotify does not have the quivalent of a live radio, so just leave with it man, just do

The Speed: I'm kinda a tech entusiast so speed in terms of seconds means a lot to me, I'm also able to detect if a website is fast or slow, which is something that has spilled over to my music . Apple music is not so fast .I'm not going to be definite on that one since I use an andriod device, but compared to Spotify, you might need some seconds before your music starts playing. Unlike spotify, where your music starts playing almost immediately. [I heard it's some romance between Python, Java and C++](https://labs.spotify.com/2013/03/20/how-we-use-python-at-spotify/)
again i have not been able to confirm if this using an Apple device soxyz.


Reccomended listens: Spotify does a great job with this one. I can -and have listened to music non stop(for over 12 hours -don't ask me what I ws doing) with amazing recommended music that I actually loved listening to. Apple music reccomended listen is trash, let's leave it like that.

The price: That's the most important one right. In NIgeria for me as a student, Apple music is far cheaper and no brainer compared to Spotify. I get charged ₦450.00 per month with my first 3 months absolutely free. On spotify, I get to pay 9 USD per month to be able to enjoy downloads and other benefits of Pro. Spotify allows you to listen to music for free interrupted with ads and multiple functions like replay, downloads or skip removed or limited.
My first 3 or 4 months on Spotify, I actually paid the 9 USD till I spoke with a friend from Belguim whom I met on twitter that allowe me to use her ID while she uses my Netflix passcode. That way, I won't have to use an escrow and also save cost, needing to subscribe to only Netflix xyz. 
That went on for some month before I switched back to my account and continued with the free tier - till now.

So it's a closely fought out war, but if you're in Nigeria, and can afford to cough out ₦450 monthly, Apple music is a no brainer, otherwise --- notjustok.com
